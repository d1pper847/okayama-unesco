Community Learning Festival 2026

A community event connecting culture, education, sustainability, and local participation in Okayama.

The Community Learning Festival brings together local residents, students, volunteers, educators, and international participants to learn about sustainable community development and cultural exchange.

Through workshops, presentations, cultural activities, and discussion sessions, participants can experience how UNESCO and ESD values are connected to everyday life in Okayama.

Event Highlights

The program includes community learning workshops, local culture activities, volunteer introductions, and opportunities for participants to connect with ongoing projects.

Participation

Anyone interested in education, culture, sustainability, or local community activities is welcome to participate. More detailed information will be announced soon.
