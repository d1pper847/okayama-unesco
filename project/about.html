Connecting Okayama with UNESCO Values

We support culture, education, sustainability, and community learning through local activities in Okayama.

Our Mission

Building a peaceful and sustainable future through learning.

The Okayama UNESCO Association connects people through education, culture, international understanding, and local community activities.

Our goal is to create opportunities where people can learn together, share experiences, and take action for a better future.
